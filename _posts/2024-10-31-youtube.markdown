I applied this knowledge in this example video edit. I found [CC PDM 1.0](https://creativecommons.org/publicdomain/mark/1.0/) music on [musopen.org](https://www.musopen.org/music/2547-piano-sonata-no-14-in-c-sharp-minor-moonlight-sonata-op-27-no-2/) and synchronised it with my CS2 clips in DaVinci Resolve.
